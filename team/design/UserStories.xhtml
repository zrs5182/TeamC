User Stories

Team C


Sections

  • Appearance
  • User Interface
  • Utility

General Requirements

  • When starting a new project, only the contention is present.

Appearance

  • Borders of claim boxes and text areas within the claims have rounded corners.
  • When editing a project, each claim contains a text area within a field (colored appropriately for support, refutation, and rebuttal), a label (identifying the claim type), and buttons to append new support and refutation claims.
  • Connectors between claims are curved and tapered.

User Interface

  • I can click on the content of aclaim and edit it.
  • A claim has separate buttons to append support and refutation claims.
  • When I create a child claim, it is joined to the parent claim by connectors of different colors and endpoints (depending on if they are support or refutation).
  • When I delete a claim, it and all of its children are removed.
  • When I delete the contention, all children are deleted and the contention text is deleted, but the contention box remains on the screen.
  • When I attempt to delete a claim that has children, I am prompted with a confirmation dialog.

Utility

  • I can save and load projects at any point.
